Alt-Generator

Alt-Generator is a powerful web application designed to automatically create descriptive alternative text (alt text) for your images. By leveraging modern AI, this tool helps developers, content creators, and website managers make their content accessible to everyone, including users who rely on screen readers.The idea for Alt-Generator was born from firsthand experience. While working as an alternative text formatter at my college's disability resource center, I saw how critical yet time-consuming it was to make digital content accessible. This project was built to automate that process, creating a tool to help myself and others ensure that the web is open to everyone.
